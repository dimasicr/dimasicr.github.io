Fibonacci Kawung

Description

Motive inspired by traditional Indonesian motive Kawung decorated with traditional isen cecek, arranged as Fibonacci Spiral. Fibonacci spiral is spiral made from Fibonacci sequence. Fibonacci sequence is a sequence in which each number is the sum of two preceeding ones.

Fibonacci sequence is named after Leonardo of Pisa, also known as Fibonacci, who introduced the sequence to Western Europen mathematics in his 1202 book Liber Abaci

Math Concept

The Fibonacci sequence can be defined as:

\(F_0 = 0 , F_1 = 1 \) , and for n > 0:

\(F_n = F_n-1 + F_n-2 \)
