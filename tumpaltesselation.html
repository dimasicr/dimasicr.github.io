Tumpal Tesselation

Description

Motive inspired by traditional Indonesian motive Tumpal decorated with traditional isen cecek, arranged in regular tessellation and semiregular tesselation grid

Tumpal is triangle shaped pattern, usually filled with isen. In this motive, Tumpal is decorated with wave like pattern that is tangent to the triangle Outline.

Math Concept

Coordinate and radius of circle tangent to the triangles can be described as:

\( x_c = 0.5 w - \frac{R}{tan(\theta/2)} \)

\( y_c = R \)

How to Draw

1. Draw perpendicular bisector of AB

2. Draw perpendicular bisector of BC

3. Draw perpendicular bisector of CA. The intersection of perpendicular bisector is the center of circle circumscribing the triangle

4. Draw N-Leaf Kawung, based on quadratic bezier curve. We can start at (0,0), mid point between vertrices and control point, and the vertices as the end point.
